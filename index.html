Goal

Bankruptcy prediction is always a challenge for investors. Given the information of the company, such as their financial condition and performance on the market, if people could dig out the useful data from all these information and examine them with proper method, they could possibly discover some clues to predict the future performance of these companies and affect the investors decisions. One of the hot topic of this predicting art is to predict if the company is going bankrupt in the near future.

Function

The user can save the different company statistics data and do technical analysis. Also, the user can see the prediction of the company bankruptcy.

Artificial Intelligence

We are using the artificial intelligence for the bankruptcy prediction. Machine learning is already being applied to so many tasks, which include mostly classification or prediction on subjects, that is very suitable for bankruptcy prediction.
